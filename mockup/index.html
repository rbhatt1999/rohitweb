Code-corridor 3D — Play Mode + Readable Text

Default behaviour is scroll mode — read the portfolio normally, the camera flies forward as you scroll. Click ▶ play in the nav to enter play mode: free-fly through the corridor with WASD + mouse-drag, collect commit tokens (lime octahedra), fly close to a project beacon (lime sphere) to discover it, and tap T any time to drop into a typed-command terminal. Pattern lifted from Bruno Simon's drivable portfolio and Thibault Introvigne's collectible spaceman.

// v4 — adds play mode: free-fly with WASD + mouse-drag, collect 24 commit tokens (lime octahedra) along the corridor, fly within 4 units of any of 6 project beacons (lime spheres) to "discover" them — the corresponding card in the portfolio pulses lime. HUD shows tokens / projects / fps / velocity / z-pos. Press T for an in-world terminal supporting help, ls, whoami, cat about, cat sadhak_ai, play snake, clear, exit. Achievement toasts on first token, first discovery, all-discovered, and snake high-score. Mobile uses arrow keys (or on a real device this'd be on-screen joystick + tap). Refs: Bruno Simon's drivable portfolio, Thibault Introvigne's collectible spaceman, JReyes MC's circuit-tour Minecraft world.
